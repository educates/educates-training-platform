---
title: Headings
---

Markdown supports six levels of headings using the `#` character. The number of
`#` characters determines the heading level.

The markdown for headings is:

~~~markdown
# Heading 1

## Heading 2

### Heading 3

#### Heading 4

##### Heading 5

###### Heading 6
~~~

The rendered headings are shown below:

# Heading 1

## Heading 2

### Heading 3

#### Heading 4

##### Heading 5

###### Heading 6
